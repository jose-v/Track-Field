üéØ What is Our Analytics Architecture?

Think of our analytics architecture as the "brain" of our Track & Field application. Just like how a coach analyzes an athlete's performance using consistent methods and proven formulas, our system uses standardized calculations to provide insights about:

  • Sleep Quality: How well athletes are recovering
  • Training Load: Whether athletes are training too hard or too easy
  • Injury Risk: Early warning signs before problems occur
  • Wellness Trends: Overall health and readiness patterns
  • Performance Tracking: Improvement measurements and goal progress
üéØ Key Point: Instead of having different parts of our app calculate things differently (like having multiple coaches with different methods), we now have ONE consistent system that everyone uses.

üèÉ‚Äç‚ôÇÔ∏è Real-World Examples

Example 1: Sleep Analysis

Scenario: Sarah logs her sleep from 10:30 PM to 6:15 AM with "Good" quality.

What Our System Does:

  1. Calculates Duration: 7 hours 45 minutes (handles midnight crossover automatically)
  2. Analyzes Quality: Converts "Good" to numerical score for trend analysis
  3. Compares to History: Checks if this is better/worse than her average
  4. Provides Insights: "Great sleep duration! Your consistency score is 85%"

Where You See This: Sleep page, dashboard card, AI chatbot, coach reports - all showing the same accurate information.

Example 2: Injury Risk Assessment

Scenario: Mike has been training hard for 3 weeks preparing for a meet.

What Our System Does:

  1. Calculates Training Load: Uses RPE √ó Duration for each workout
  2. Computes ACWR: Compares recent training (acute) to long-term average (chronic)
  3. Assesses Risk: ACWR of 1.6 = "High Risk" zone
  4. Recommends Action: "Reduce training intensity - consider active recovery"

Business Value: Prevents injuries before they happen, keeping athletes healthy and competitive.

Example 3: Wellness Trends

Scenario: Emma fills out daily wellness surveys for 2 weeks.

What Our System Does:

  1. Weighted Scoring: Combines fatigue, stress, motivation with research-based weights
  2. Trend Detection: Uses statistical analysis to identify if wellness is improving/declining
  3. Red Flag Alerts: Automatically flags concerning patterns
  4. Personalized Advice: "Your stress levels are elevated - try meditation techniques"

Coach Benefit: Early warning system for athlete burnout or overtraining.
