Core Principles

Simplicity & Transparency

Easy-to-understand tiers with clear value. No hidden fees or complex credit systems for AI usage.

Value-Based

Pricing reflects the features and benefits provided, especially the integrated AI assistance.

Scalability

Tiers cater to individuals, growing coaches, and larger teams/organizations.

AI as an Integrated Benefit

AI features are part of the value proposition of each tier, with more advanced capabilities unlocked at higher tiers.

Tier 1: Athlete (Unattached)
Individual athletes training independently
Core Value Proposition: Access to a smart training log, personal performance analytics, and AI-driven insights to optimize their individual training and recovery.

Pricing Logic

Monthly: $7 - $15
Annually: $70 - $150
Student Discount: 25-40% off for verified students

Key Features

  • Full access to personal workout logging
  • Personal performance tracking and analytics dashboards
  • Personalized AI assistant for training insights
  • Full gamification features (points, badges, streaks)
  • Ability to be "invited" by a coach
AI Value Proposition: AI acts as a personal performance and wellness advisor.
Tier 2: Coach
Individual coaches managing one or more athletes
Core Value Proposition: Powerful tools to create training plans, manage athletes efficiently, analyze performance, monitor injury risk, and leverage AI to enhance coaching effectiveness.

Pricing Logic

Base Monthly: $29 - $49 (includes 1 athlete slot)
Per Additional Athlete: $5/month
Scales with the coach's business growth

Key Features

  • All Athlete tier features for coach's own use
  • Workout Template Creation & Management
  • Athlete Roster Management
  • Training Plan Assignment
  • Comprehensive Athlete Analytics
  • Coach-Focused AI Assistant
  • Communication tools for athletes
AI Value Proposition: AI acts as a coaching assistant, providing deeper insights across their roster and saving time on analysis and planning.
Tier 3: Team
Schools, Universities, Clubs
Core Value Proposition: A centralized platform for managing the entire track and field program, enabling consistent training methodologies, team-wide oversight, and advanced analytics.

Sub-Tiers

Team Starter Up to 3 Coaches, 30 Athletes
$79 - $129/month
Team Growth Up to 7 Coaches, 75 Athletes
$149 - $249/month
Team Pro Up to 15 Coaches, 200 Athletes
$299 - $499/month
Enterprise Custom pricing for
larger institutions

Key Features

  • All Coach tier features for each coach seat
  • Administrative Dashboard
  • Team-Wide Analytics & Reporting
  • Centralized Communication Tools
  • Shared asset libraries
  • Custom branding options
AI Value Proposition: AI provides insights at an organizational level, helps identify team-wide trends, and supports program-wide consistency.

General Considerations for All Tiers

Free Trial

14-day or 30-day free trial for "Coach" and "Team Starter" tiers to experience full value, especially AI features.

Annual Discount

10-20% discount (effectively 1-2 months free) for users who subscribe annually.

AI Usage Clarity

Clear marketing that AI is integral to each tier's value. Monitor API costs to ensure pricing sustainability.

Stripe Implementation Strategy

Athlete & Coach Base

Standard subscriptions with quantity-based pricing for Coach tier's per-athlete add-ons using Stripe's metered billing capabilities.

Team Tiers

Separate subscription products in Stripe, each with defined pricing and included coach/athlete limits.

Technical Requirements

  • Robust subscription status checking logic
  • Row Level Security (RLS) in Supabase
  • Frontend entitlement verification
  • Usage tracking for AI features
  • Billing administration tools
