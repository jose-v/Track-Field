üéØ Implementation Strategy

Recommended Approach:

  • Week 1-2: Focus exclusively on Critical priority items
  • Week 3-6: Begin High priority features while maintaining critical systems
  • Month 2-3: Implement Medium priority features based on user feedback
  • Month 4+: Evaluate Low priority items and plan Future features

Success Metrics: User registration rate, daily active users, workout logging frequency, coach adoption rate, and user retention after 30 days.
