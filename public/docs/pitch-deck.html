The Problem

Challenges in Track & Field Training

Fragmented Data

Athletes and coaches struggle with scattered training data across multiple platforms, making it difficult to gain comprehensive performance insights.

Limited Analytics

Existing solutions offer basic tracking but lack sophisticated analytics to optimize training and prevent injuries.

Communication Gaps

Inefficient communication between athletes, coaches, and team managers leads to misaligned training objectives.

Low Engagement

Traditional training apps struggle with user retention and consistent engagement, hindering long-term athletic development.
